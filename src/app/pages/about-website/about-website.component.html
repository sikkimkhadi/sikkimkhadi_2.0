About This Website

Purpose

This website serves as the official digital platform for Sikkim Khadi and Village Industries Board (SKVIB), providing comprehensive information about our organization, programs, and services to the public.

Features

  • Information about SKVIB's mission and objectives
  • Details about our products and services
  • Latest news and announcements
  • Contact information and office locations
  • Important documents and policies
  • Gallery showcasing our work and events

Technology

This website is built using modern web technologies to ensure:

  • Fast loading times and optimal performance
  • Mobile-responsive design for all devices
  • Accessibility compliance for all users
  • Search engine optimization
  • Secure data transmission

Accessibility

We are committed to making our website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. The website follows web accessibility guidelines and includes features such as:

  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • High contrast text and colors
  • Alternative text for images
